## Current Research Focus

- Quantum Reservoir Computing with strict classical, feature-budget, and shot-budget controls
- Quantum Machine Learning benchmarks where claims, baselines, and evidence stay auditable
- Time-series forecasting from quantum measurement records and controlled reservoir features
- Reproducible research software: scripts, manifests, cached artifacts, and publication figures
- Quantum generative modeling, parametrized Hamiltonians, and optimization workflows

## Open Research

Public research repositories are kept narrow, reproducible, and evidence-bounded.

- [QRC Operating Band](https://github.com/eybmits/qrc-operating-band): reproducibility package for "Where a Quantum Reservoir Works: A Transferable Operating Band".
- [QC Symmetry](https://github.com/eybmits/qc_symmetry): public reproducibility package for symmetry-focused quantum-computing experiments.
- [IQP Generative Modeling](https://github.com/eybmits/iqp_generative): frozen artifacts and figure pipelines for parity-structured quantum generative modeling.
- [Parametrized Hamiltonians](https://github.com/eybmits/parameterized-hamiltonians-id): implicit-differentiation experiments for bilevel Max-Cut optimization.
- [Statistically Learnable QRC](https://github.com/eybmits/QRC-statistically): archival research project on statistically learnable Quantum Reservoir Computing.
